Key Learning Points

  • Goal-Conditioned Approach: Single model for multiple views/goals - addresses scalability
  • Generalization Emphasis: CPB and data-augmented loss for anatomical variations across patients
  • Simulation-Based Training: Large-scale CT dataset for training, real-world validation
  • Clinical Validation: Navigation to both standard and interventional views (e.g., LAA closure)
  • Quantitative Metrics: Position and angle errors as domain-relevant evaluation criteria
  • Scalability: Single model vs. models trained on individual views
