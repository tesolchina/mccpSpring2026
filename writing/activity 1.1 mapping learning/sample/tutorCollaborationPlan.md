# Tutor Collaboration Plan: Developing Writing and AI Skills

**Student Profile:** Computer Science PhD Student with technical writing strengths but needs help with academic narrative and literature synthesis
**Tutor:** Dr. Simon Wang (English language specialist with AI interests)
**Collaboration Focus:** Building partnership between student, AI tools, and language instructor

## Short-term Goals (Next 2 Weeks)
### Writing Skills Development
- **Target Area:** Literature synthesis and critical analysis (Move 2 in Literature Review)
- **Tutor Sessions:** Weekly 30-minute meetings to review drafts and provide feedback
- **Student Preparation:** Submit 300-word literature synthesis sample before each session
- **AI Integration:** Use AI to generate initial synthesis attempts, then discuss with tutor

### AI Skills Development
- **Current Level:** Basic prompt writing and code assistance
- **Target Skills:** Academic writing prompts, citation management, style checking
- **Learning Activities:** Practice writing prompts for different writing moves
- **Tutor Role:** Demonstrate effective AI prompts and review student's AI outputs

## Medium-term Goals (Semester)
### Personalized Learning Approach
- **Small Group Sessions:** Join 3-4 student group meetings for peer feedback
- **Skill Tracking:** Maintain a learning journal documenting writing improvements
- **AI Exploration:** Experiment with different AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Grammarly) for various writing tasks

### Research Writing Mastery
- **CILO Alignment:**
  - CILO 2: Apply thesis/article structures in own writing
  - CILO 3: Build repertoire of linguistic features
  - CILO 4: Strategic citation practices
- **Assessment Integration:** Use assignment feedback to identify specific improvement areas
- **Tutor Feedback Loop:** Regular review of writing samples with focus on rhetorical moves

## Long-term Partnership Vision
### AI-Language Teacher Collaboration
- **Student Role:** Active participant in exploring AI-assisted writing pedagogy
- **Tutor Research:** Contribute to Dr. Wang's exploration of AI agents in academic writing
- **Mutual Learning:** Student shares technical insights, tutor provides language expertise

### Skill Transfer
- **Beyond Course:** Develop sustainable writing habits using AI tools
- **Professional Development:** Build skills applicable to PhD thesis and journal publications
- **Teaching Philosophy:** Understand how AI can enhance rather than replace traditional writing instruction

## Communication Plan
- **Frequency:** Weekly check-ins via email/chat with draft submissions
- **Format:** Combination of individual meetings and small group workshops
- **Documentation:** Keep records of AI prompts, tutor feedback, and writing improvements
- **Success Metrics:** Improved assignment grades, increased confidence in academic writing, effective AI tool usage

## Expected Outcomes
- Complete writing assignment successfully (40% course weighting)
- Develop personalized AI-assisted writing workflow
- Build lasting collaboration with language instructor for ongoing support
- Contribute to innovative approaches in academic writing pedagogy
