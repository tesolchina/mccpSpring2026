# Assignment Completion Plan with AI Assistance

**Student:** Computer Science PhD Student (Machine Learning focus)
**Time Allocation:** 3 hours maximum with AI assistance
**Assignment:** Writing an Introduction and Literature Review (1000-1500 words)

## Phase 1: Preparation and Research (45 minutes)
- **AI Tool:** Use ChatGPT/Claude for initial research questions and keyword generation
- **Task:** Identify 8-10 relevant papers from Google Scholar and IEEE Xplore
- **AI Assistance:** "Help me find recent papers on [my topic] that discuss [specific gap]"
- **Goal:** Create annotated bibliography with key findings and methodologies

## Phase 2: Structural Planning (30 minutes)
- **AI Tool:** Use AI writing assistants (Grammarly, Jasper, or built-in IDE tools)
- **Task:** Map out the rhetorical moves for Introduction and Literature Review
- **AI Assistance:** "Help me structure an academic introduction following Swales' CARS model"
- **Goal:** Create detailed outline with specific moves and transitions

## Phase 3: Draft Writing (60 minutes)
- **AI Tool:** Use AI for drafting specific sections, not entire papers
- **Task:** Write first drafts of each move, focusing on one section at a time
- **AI Assistance:** "Help me write a centrality statement for [my research area]" or "Suggest transitions between paragraphs"
- **Goal:** Complete full first draft with citations

## Phase 4: Revision and Refinement (45 minutes)
- **AI Tool:** Use AI for proofreading and style suggestions
- **Task:** Revise for clarity, academic tone, and coherence
- **AI Assistance:** "Check if this paragraph uses appropriate academic hedging" or "Suggest more precise terminology"
- **Goal:** Final polished draft ready for tutor review

## Success Metrics
- Word count: 1000-1500 words
- All rhetorical moves clearly indicated
- Proper citations and academic language
- Coherent research narrative

## AI Usage Guidelines
- Use AI as a writing assistant, not a replacement for thinking
- Always verify AI suggestions against course materials
- Document AI interactions for learning purposes
- Focus on understanding rather than just producing text
